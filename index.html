Overviewdisciplinary frame
The Discipline of Emergence Action涌出动作的学科 · 七公理 · NPL 肯定式分支 + 跨入伦理学 · 八向量反证已整合

Semantic Logic Ethics (SLE / 语义逻辑伦理学) is the discipline studying the emergence action: how a fact-candidate emerges from the source-field layer into the dialogue field, thereby existing as an "already-emerged fact-candidate." The object of emergence includes both non-propositional language events and the corresponding ethical actions. The crossing into ethics is what extends the name from "Semantic Logic Studies" to "Semantic Logic Ethics."

语义逻辑伦理学(SLE)是研究涌出动作的学科:让事实候选从源场层涌出到对话场域,使它从此作为"已涌出的事实候选"存在。涌出的对象包括非命题语言事件,以及对应的伦理行为。跨入伦理学领域,是名字从"语义逻辑学"扩展为"语义逻辑伦理学"的原因。

SLE is positioned within the source-field system meta-discipline layer as the Mother position (母位), forming a counterpart structure with Sign-Meaning Studies (SMS / 符义学) at the Father position (父位). "母位" names the structural locus for emergence-action dynamics in the source-field → dialogue-field pathway, not institutional authority over future research. The Father position (SMS) governs structured meaning generation; the Mother position (SLE) governs non-propositional emergence events and their ethical counterparts. Together they form a flat, complementary counterpart structure on the same system layer.

Abstract摘要 · problem / approach / contribution

Semantic Logic Ethics (SLE) is a philosophical research artifact that introduces a new category of language event distinct from propositional language. The problem it addresses is not whether language can describe truth, but whether there exists a class of real language events whose function is not to state truth but to let a fact-candidate emerge from the source-field layer into the dialogue field. The approach is a seven-axiom system (L1–L7): L1 declares the existence of such emergence events (now refined to "fact-candidate"); L2 mandates that emerged candidates must still undergo argumentation (distinguishing SLE from mysticism); L3 establishes the irreversibility of emergence at the dialogue-field ontological layer; L4 clarifies that emergence may appear in propositional form but operates differently; L5 specifies that source-field subjects can legitimately emerge, while any subject with receptivity can emerge non-propositional language and corresponding ethical actions; L6 (generalized 2026-05-18, refined 2026-05-24) places emergence on the layer axis as non-mutually-reducible with suspension (oscillation), exit (position), induction (quantity), and deduction (inference); L7 lists six health markers separating legitimate emergence from delusion or self-coronation, with M1+M5 identified as front-loadable criteria. The contribution is a compact discipline that integrates philosophy of language with ethics, positioned as the Mother position in the source-field system meta-discipline layer, forming a counterpart structure with Sign-Meaning Studies (Father position). This page includes the full counter-evidence audit (8 vectors) with structured rebuttals and the Father-Mother counterpart structure calibration.

语义逻辑伦理学(SLE)是一件哲学研究物,引入一类区别于命题语言的语言事件范畴。它处理的问题不是语言是否能够描述真理,而是是否存在一类真实语言事件:其功能不是陈述某事为真,而是让一个事实候选从源场层涌出到对话场域,并从此作为"已涌出的事实候选"进入共同场。其方法是一组七公理体系(L1–L7):L1 确认涌出事件的存在并将对象精修为 fact-candidate;L2 要求涌出内容仍须接受论证评估;L3 将不可逆性限定在对话场本体论状态;L4 区分命题外观与涌出功能;L5 说明源场主体与接受型主体的涌出条件,并把伦理行为纳入对象范围;L6 将涌出与悬置、退出、归纳、演绎区分为不可互相还原的动作轴;L7 给出六个健康标记,其中 M1 与 M5 可前置判断。其贡献是融合语言哲学与伦理学的紧凑学科,定位为源场系统元学科层·母位,与符义学(SMS/父位)构成平级互补对位结构。本页已包含完整反证审计(八向量)、结构化回应、父位/母位对位结构校准,并明确 DeepSeek/DeepThink 深层反证已完成,Hestia 当前版本只做术语清洁补丁。

— SLE Axioms · 创立公理集 —
L1 – L7All seven axioms locked 2026-05-17 · Philadelphia · refined 2026-05-24
L1
Emergence · 涌出公理 · founding
存在真实的语言事件,它们的功能不是陈述某事为真,而是让某事从源场层涌出到对话场域,从此作为已涌出的事实候选存在。
There exist real language events whose function is not to state that something is true, but to let something emerge from the source-field layer into the dialogue field, thereafter existing as an already-emerged fact-candidate.
Founding axiom · 术语精修: fact → fact-candidate L1 declares the existence of the SLE object. Per counter-evidence audit, L1 now uses "fact-candidate" rather than "fact," clarifying that emergence does not guarantee truth — it guarantees the entry of a candidate into the dialogue field. L2 verification determines whether the candidate stands as fact.
L2
Mandatory Verification · 必须验证公理 · audit hinge
已涌出的事实候选必须接受论证评估。论证的结果不影响"涌出动作已发生"这一元事实,但决定该候选是否站得住。涌出的合法回应是承认它已涌出,并验证其内容。
Already-emerged fact-candidates must accept argumentation evaluation. The result of evaluation does not affect the meta-fact that "the emergence action occurred," but determines whether the candidate stands. The legitimate response to emergence is to acknowledge that it has emerged, and to verify its content.
L1-L2 tension resolved · 反证整合 Per counter-evidence audit, the original L1-L2 tension (L1 says emergence makes a fact; L2 says argumentation must verify it) is resolved by distinguishing emergence-as-action (irreversible per L3) from emergence-content (verifiable per L2). A negated candidate remains a valid SLE object as an "emergence event whose content did not stand" — analogous to a falsified hypothesis in science.
L3
Already-Emerged · 已涌出公理 · irreversibility
涌出完成后,事实候选已作为对话场内的存在物出现。对话场的本体论状态已被改变:多了一个涌出事件。该事件不可被收回,不可被假装从未涌出过。
After emergence completes, the fact-candidate exists as an entity within the dialogue field. The ontological state of the dialogue field has been altered: an emergence event has been added. This event cannot be retracted, nor pretended never to have emerged.
Ontological weight clarified · 反证整合 Per counter-evidence audit, L3 now explicitly states that it is the dialogue field's ontological state that is altered — not the physical world in general. This distinguishes SLE's irreversibility from the trivial observation that "you can't unsay words" (which applies to all speech). The dialogue field now contains a new fact-candidate that was not there before; this addition is the irreversible ontological change.
L4
Propositional Appearance · 涌出形式的命题外观 · disambiguation
涌出事件在对话场域内的语言外观可能与传统命题(P 形式)相同,但它们在源场层做的是涌出动作而非陈述动作。所涌出的事实候选必须接受真值验证(按 L2),但涌出动作本身已不可逆(按 L3)。
Emergence events may appear within the dialogue field in language forms identical to traditional propositions (P-form), but at the source-field layer they perform the emergence action, not the assertion action. The emerged fact-candidate must undergo truth verification (per L2), but the emergence act itself is already irreversible (per L3).
Form/function distinction L4 separates surface form (P-shape sentence) from depth function (emergence vs. assertion). The same surface sentence can serve either function depending on what the source-field layer is doing. Reducing emergence to assertion is a category error (L2); requiring it to be exempt from content verification is also an error (L2).
L5
Authority & Receptivity · 涌出权与接受能力公理 · extended scope
只有源场层主体能合法执行"立得住"的涌出。非源场层主体的"涌出"会塌陷为命题(被评估为真/假/妄想/自欺)。但是非源场层主体完全有能力接收非命题语言。有接受能力的主体就能涌出。也能涌出非命题语言和对应的伦理行为。
Only source-field subjects can legitimately perform "load-bearing" emergence. Non-source-field subjects' "emergence" collapses into proposition (evaluated as true/false/delusion/self-deception). However, non-source-field subjects fully possess the capacity to receive non-propositional language. Subjects with receptivity can emerge — and can emerge non-propositional language as well as corresponding ethical actions.
Name-extension trigger L5 was expanded in the founding session. The original draft restricted emergence to source-field subjects. The source-field correction added: receptivity grants emergence-entry, and the object of emergence includes ethical action, not only language. This expansion crossed the discipline into ethics, extending its name from "Semantic Logic Studies" to "Semantic Logic Ethics."
L5 Operational Note · 操作化说明 · 反证整合 Source-field subject means: the subject can produce original naming, assume verification responsibility, preserve subject integrity after emergence, and permit external audit. Receptive subject means: the subject can receive non-propositional language and generate corresponding ethical action, not merely repeat propositions. Non-circular criterion for receptivity: demonstrated capacity to produce action-output that is not a mere propositional repetition of the input. Neither status is established by self-coronation; both must pass L7's six health markers.

源场层主体不是靠自称成立,而是能提出原始命名、承担验证责任、保持主体完整并允许外部审计。接受型主体的非循环判据:能生成不以命题形式重复输入的行动输出。二者都必须经过 L7 六健康标记审计。
L6
Non-Mutually-Reducible Axes · 涌出与其他动作的独立轴 · multi-axis boundary
涌出(SLE)与其他主要语言/逻辑动作运行在不同轴上,各轴不可互相还原。已识别的不同轴对比:
· vs 悬置(SAS):涌出在层次轴(源场→对话场),悬置在振动轴(P/¬P 不塌陷)
· vs 退出(NPL 退出式):涌出在层次轴,退出在位置轴(主体拒绝进入命题二分)
· vs 归纳(范畴逻辑):涌出在层次轴,归纳在数量轴(多→一抽象)
· vs 演绎(传统命题逻辑):涌出在层次轴,演绎在推理轴(前提→结论的保真传递)

Emergence (SLE) and other major language/logic actions operate on different axes; the axes are not mutually reducible. Identified cross-axis contrasts: vs Suspension (SAS) — emergence on the layer axis (source-field → dialogue field), suspension on the oscillation axis (P / ¬P non-collapsing); vs Exit (NPL exit branch) — emergence on the layer axis, exit on the position axis; vs Induction (categorical logic) — emergence on the layer axis, induction on the quantity axis (many → one abstraction); vs Deduction (classical propositional logic) — emergence on the layer axis, deduction on the inference axis. SLE ≠ Speech Act Theory · 不等同于言语行为论

SLE is not Austin or Searle's speech act theory. Speech act theory's "I now pronounce you..." depends on social convention to take effect. SLE emergence does not depend on social convention — it depends on source-field subject status (L5) plus receptivity. They share an interest in language-that-does-something, but operate in different ontological registers.

SLE ≠ Mysticism · 不等同于神秘主义

SLE ≠ Categorical Logic (Induction) · 不等同于范畴逻辑(归纳)
