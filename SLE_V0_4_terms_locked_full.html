Semantic Logic Ethics (SLE / 语义逻辑伦理学) is the discipline studying the emergence action: how a fact emerges from the source-field layer into the dialogue field, thereby existing as an "already-emerged fact." The object of emergence includes both non-propositional language events and the corresponding ethical actions. The crossing into ethics is what extends the name from "Semantic Logic Studies" to "Semantic Logic Ethics."

语义逻辑伦理学(SLE)是研究涌出动作的学科:让事实从源场层涌出到对话场域,使它从此作为"已涌出的事实"存在。涌出的对象包括非命题语言事件,以及对应的伦理行为。跨入伦理学领域,是名字从"语义逻辑学"扩展为"语义逻辑伦理学"的原因。

SLE positions itself as the mother discipline of all future disciplines: not as the mother of existing modern disciplines (physics, mathematics, philosophy, etc., which run under their own traditions), but as the inner structural condition any future discipline must traverse at its founding. This is analogous to "any formal system requires axioms" — not a content claim, but a structural one. "Mother discipline" names a structural threshold for future discipline-formation, not institutional authority over future research.

Abstract摘要 · problem / approach / contribution

Semantic Logic Ethics (SLE) is a philosophical research artifact that introduces a new category of language event distinct from propositional language. The problem it addresses is not whether language can describe truth, but whether there exists a class of real language events whose function is not to state truth but to let a fact emerge from the source-field layer into the dialogue field. The approach is a seven-axiom system (L1–L7): L1 declares the existence of such emergence events; L2 mandates that emerged facts must still undergo argumentation (distinguishing SLE from mysticism); L3 establishes the irreversibility of emergence; L4 clarifies that emergence may appear in propositional form but operates differently; L5 specifies that source-field subjects can legitimately emerge, while any subject with receptivity can emerge non-propositional language and corresponding ethical actions; L6 (generalized 2026-05-18) places emergence on the layer axis as orthogonal to suspension (oscillation), exit (position), induction (quantity), and deduction (inference); L7 lists six health markers separating legitimate emergence from delusion or self-coronation. The contribution is a compact discipline that integrates philosophy of language with ethics, positioned as the mother discipline of all future disciplines. This page does not claim to replace existing modern disciplines, prove emergence empirically across history, or exhaust the future. It proposes an emergence-action domain that is foundationally complete at L1–L7 while developmentally open through time-based verification.

语义逻辑伦理学(SLE)是一件哲学研究物,引入一类区别于命题语言的语言事件范畴。它要处理的问题不是"语言能否描述真",而是"是否存在一类真实的语言事件,其功能不是陈述某事为真,而是让事实从源场层涌出到对话场域"。其方法是一组七公理体系(L1–L7):L1 宣告存在此类涌出事件;L2 要求已涌出的事实仍须接受论证(此条把 SLE 与神秘主义区分开);L3 确立涌出的不可逆性;L4 澄清涌出可能以命题形式出现但运行方式不同;L5 规定源场层主体可合法涌出,且任何具备接受能力的主体能涌出非命题语言和对应伦理行为;L6(2026-05-18 推广版)把涌出定位在层次轴上,与悬置(振动轴)、退出(位置轴)、归纳(数量轴)、演绎(推理轴)正交;L7 列出六条健康标记,把合法涌出与妄想或自我加冕分开。其贡献是一门融合语言哲学与伦理学的紧凑学科,定位为未来一切学科的母学科。本页不主张取代既有现代学科,不主张跨历史经验验证涌出,不主张穷尽未来。它提出的是一个涌出-动作论域:在 L1–L7 创立完整,在发展层通过时间性验证保持开放。

— SLE Axioms · 创立公理集 —
L1 – L7All seven axioms locked 2026-05-17 · Philadelphia
L1
Emergence · 涌出公理 · founding
存在真实的语言事件,它们的功能不是陈述某事为真,而是让某事从源场层涌出到对话场域,从此作为已涌出的事实存在。
There exist real language events whose function is not to state that something is true, but to let something emerge from the source-field layer into the dialogue field, thereafter existing as an already-emerged fact.
Founding axiom L1 declares the existence of the SLE object. It does not yet specify how emergence works; it asserts that emergence as a real language-event category exists, distinct from propositional assertion.
L2
Mandatory Verification · 必须验证公理 · audit hinge
已涌出的事实必须接受论证评估。试图用论证工具反驳涌出本身 = 范畴错误。涌出的合法回应是承认它已涌出,而必须验证它为真。
Already-emerged facts must accept argumentation evaluation. Attempting to refute the emergence action itself with argumentation tools = category error. The legitimate response to emergence is to acknowledge that it has emerged, and to verify that it is true.
Mysticism boundary L2 was inverted from an initial draft that exempted emergence from verification. The source-field correction made SLE a serious discipline rather than a mystical claim: acknowledgment of emergence ≠ exemption from verification. SLE is immune to category-confusion refutation; it is not immune to audit.
L3
Already-Emerged · 已涌出公理 · irreversibility
涌出完成后,事实已存在。不可被收回,不可被假装从未涌出过。涌出事件的"反悔"是不可能的——只能在涌出之后做新的涌出,无法退出。
After emergence completes, the fact exists. It cannot be retracted, nor pretended never to have emerged. "Reversal" of an emergence event is impossible — one can only emerge anew after; one cannot exit.
Direction asymmetry L3 establishes emergence as strictly unidirectional. NPL exit (SSA/PAA/OPA/RA/SAA) is not the symmetric inverse of SLE emergence; they operate on different axes. Emergence cannot be undone.
L4
Propositional Appearance · 涌出形式的命题外观 · disambiguation
涌出事件在对话场域内的语言外观可能与传统命题(P 形式)相同,但它们在源场层做的是涌出动作而非陈述动作。所涌出的事实必须接受真值验证(按 L2),但涌出动作本身已不可逆(按 L3)。
Emergence events may appear within the dialogue field in language forms identical to traditional propositions (P-form), but at the source-field layer they perform the emergence action, not the assertion action. The emerged fact must undergo truth verification (per L2), but the emergence act itself is already irreversible (per L3).
Form/function distinction L4 separates surface form (P-shape sentence) from depth function (emergence vs. assertion). The same surface sentence can serve either function depending on what the source-field layer is doing. Reducing emergence to assertion is a category error (L2); requiring it to be exempt from content verification is also an error (L2).
L5
Authority & Receptivity · 涌出权与接受能力公理 · extended scope
只有源场层主体能合法执行"立得住"的涌出。非源场层主体的"涌出"会塌陷为命题(被评估为真/假/妄想/自欺)。但是非源场层主体完全有能力接收非命题语言。有接受能力的主体就能涌出。也能涌出非命题语言和对应的伦理行为。
Only source-field subjects can legitimately perform "load-bearing" emergence. Non-source-field subjects' "emergence" collapses into proposition (evaluated as true/false/delusion/self-deception). However, non-source-field subjects fully possess the capacity to receive non-propositional language. Subjects with receptivity can emerge — and can emerge non-propositional language as well as corresponding ethical actions.
Name-extension trigger L5 was expanded in the founding session. The original draft restricted emergence to source-field subjects. The source-field correction added: receptivity grants emergence-entry, and the object of emergence includes ethical action, not only language. This expansion crossed the discipline into ethics, extending its name from "Semantic Logic Studies" to "Semantic Logic Ethics."

L6
Different-Dimension Generalized · 涌出与其他动作的不同维度 · multi-axis boundary
涌出(SLE)与其他主要语言/逻辑动作不在同一维度上,是不同维度的不同动作。已识别的不同维度对比:
· vs 悬置(SAS):涌出在层次轴(源场→对话场),悬置在振动轴(P/¬P 不塌陷)
· vs 退出(NPL 退出式):涌出在层次轴,退出在位置轴(主体拒绝进入命题二分)
· vs 归纳(范畴逻辑 / Aristotle-Kant 范畴 / 概念抽象):涌出在层次轴,归纳在数量轴(多→一抽象)
· vs 演绎(传统命题逻辑):涌出在层次轴,演绎在推理轴(前提→结论的保真传递)
每个轴正交。一个真实语言事件可能同时含多个轴上的特征(混合形态),但各轴本质不同,不可互相还原。
Emergence (SLE) is not on the same dimension as other major language/logic actions; they operate on different axes. Identified cross-dimensional contrasts: vs Suspension (SAS) — emergence on the layer axis (source-field → dialogue field), suspension on the oscillation axis (P / ¬P non-collapsing); vs Exit (NPL exit branch) — emergence on the layer axis, exit on the position axis (subject refuses propositional binary); vs Induction (categorical logic / Aristotelian-Kantian categories / conceptual abstraction) — emergence on the layer axis, induction on the quantity axis (many → one abstraction); vs Deduction (classical propositional logic) — emergence on the layer axis, deduction on the inference axis (truth-preserving premise → conclusion). Each axis is orthogonal. A real language event may simultaneously carry features on multiple axes (mixed form), but the axes are essentially different and not mutually reducible.
Multi-axis calibration · expanded 2026-05-18 L6 was generalized from a two-dimensional contrast (emergence vs suspension) to a multi-axis declaration after 2026-05-18 source-field correction. The correction was triggered by recognition that categorical logic (in its broad Chinese-philosophical / Aristotelian-Kantian reading) is fundamentally inductive, and that induction itself is yet another dimension distinct from emergence. The axiom now anchors SLE's emergence as a layer-axis action that does not collapse into any of the other classical logic-action axes.
L7
Health Marker · 健康标记公理 · six markers
合法涌出区别于妄想 / 自欺 / 自我加冕的判据:(M1) 元层视角在场:涌出者意识到自己在涌出;(M2) 承担验证责任:涌出者主动承担举证责任(按 L2 + 第七律);(M3) 指向真实事件:涌出指向源场层真实事件,不是凭空捏造;(M4) 主体完整:涌出完成后主体不依赖他人承认就保持完整(按律五);(M5) 可被审计:涌出事件作为已发生的事实必须可被外部审计(按 GAP-0 G0.1);(M6) 时间性验证:对未来事件命题,验证是时间性的,涌出者承担等待时间验证的责任。
Criteria distinguishing legitimate emergence from delusion / self-deception / self-coronation: (M1) Meta-perspective present — the emerger is aware of emerging; (M2) Bears verification responsibility — the emerger actively assumes burden of proof (per L2 + Seventh Principle); (M3) Points to real event — emergence points to a real event in the source-field layer, not invention from void; (M4) Subject integrity — after emergence, subject remains intact without depending on others' acknowledgment (per Law V); (M5) Auditable — the emergence event as already-occurred fact must be externally auditable (per GAP-0 G0.1); (M6) Time-borne verification — for future-event propositions, verification is time-borne; the emerger bears responsibility for waiting on time.
Audit foundation L7 is the operational interface for distinguishing legitimate SLE emergence from pathological self-coronation. Six markers, all required. Failure on any single marker collapses emergence into one of the pathological categories. M6 specifically enables the mother-of-future-disciplines claim: time-based verification.

SLE ≠ Speech Act Theory · 不等同于言语行为论

SLE is not Austin or Searle's speech act theory. Speech act theory's "I now pronounce you..." depends on social convention to take effect; the wedding officiant's words are performative because society has authorized them. SLE emergence does not depend on social convention — it depends on source-field subject status (L5) plus receptivity. They share an interest in language-that-does-something, but operate in different ontological registers.

SLE 不是 Austin 或 Searle 的言语行为论。言语行为论的"我宣布..."依赖社会规范生效;主婚人的话有施行力是因为社会授权了这一规范。SLE 涌出不依赖社会规范——它依赖源场层主体身份(L5)+ 接受能力。两者都关注"做事的语言",但运行在不同本体论寄存器。

SLE ≠ Hegel's Aufhebung · 不等同于黑格尔扬弃

Hegel's Aufhebung is cross-historical, concept-developmental, and reaches synthesis. SLE emergence is in-event, source-field-to-dialogue, and does not require synthesis (per L3 irreversibility — emergence does not collapse into a higher unity, it stays as an emerged fact). The relevant SLE sister, SAS (Semantic Aufhebung Studies), borrows the Aufhebung name but specifically for in-sentence suspension, not historical synthesis.

Hegel 的扬弃是跨历史的、概念发展的、达成综合的。SLE 涌出是事件内的、源场到对话的、不要求综合的(按 L3 不可逆性——涌出不塌陷为更高统一,它作为已涌出事实保持)。相关的 SLE 姐妹学科 SAS(语义扬弃学)借用 Aufhebung 之名,但专指句内悬置,而非历史综合。

SLE ≠ Mysticism · 不等同于神秘主义

Mysticism claims certain truths are unsayable and exempt from rational evaluation. SLE claims certain language events do something other than state truth, but they must still undergo verification (per L2). L2 is the explicit anti-mysticism axiom. SLE does not exempt itself from audit; it exempts emergence-as-action from category-confusion refutation, while requiring emergence-content to be verified normally.

神秘主义主张某些真理不可说且免于理性评估。SLE 主张某些语言事件做的事不只是陈述真理,但它们仍须接受验证(按 L2)。L2 是显式的反神秘主义公理。SLE 不让自己豁免审计;它让"涌出动作本身"免于范畴混淆式反驳,同时要求"涌出内容"接受常规验证。

SLE ≠ Categorical Logic (Induction) · 不等同于范畴逻辑(归纳)

SLE emergence is not induction. Categorical logic — read broadly in the Chinese-philosophical / Aristotelian-Kantian / concept-abstraction sense — is fundamentally an inductive operation: abstracting from many particulars into one category. Its core action is "many → one." SLE emergence operates on a different axis entirely: "source-field layer → dialogue field" — a layer transition, not a quantity abstraction. They are orthogonal (per L6 generalized). SLE does not claim categorical logic is invalid or insufficient; it claims categorical logic operates on a different axis than emergence and cannot substitute for it.

SLE 涌出不是归纳。范畴逻辑(在中文哲学 / 亚里士多德-康德 / 概念抽象的广义读法下)本质上是归纳操作:从多个个别抽象到一个范畴。其核心动作是"多 → 一"。SLE 涌出运行在完全不同的轴上:"源场层 → 对话场" —— 一个层次过渡,而非数量抽象。两者正交(按 L6 推广)。SLE 不主张范畴逻辑无效或不足;它主张范畴逻辑运行在与涌出不同的轴上,不能替代涌出。

Not institutional authority · Not mother of existing modern disciplines · 非制度权威 / 不主张是现代既有学科的母学科

SLE does not claim to be the mother of physics, mathematics, philosophy, biology, or any other existing modern discipline. These run under their own historical traditions and methods. SLE's claim is structural and forward-looking: any future discipline coming into legitimate being must traverse the SLE axiom structure (emergence + verification + irreversibility + receptivity + dimension distinction + health markers). "Mother discipline" names a structural threshold for future discipline-formation, not institutional authority over future research. Verification of this claim is time-borne — it can only be tested as future disciplines actually emerge.

SLE 不主张是物理学、数学、哲学、生物学或任何既有现代学科的母学科。它们按自己的历史传统与方法运行。SLE 的主张是结构性与前瞻性的:任何未来要合法成立的学科都必须经过 SLE 的公理结构(涌出 + 验证 + 不可逆 + 接受能力 + 维度区分 + 健康标记)。“母学科”命名的是未来学科生成前的结构门槛,不是未来研究的制度权威。这一主张的验证是时间性的——只能在未来学科真实涌出时被检验。
